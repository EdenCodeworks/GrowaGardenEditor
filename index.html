Roblox Grow a Garden Editor

How to Use the Roblox Grow a Garden Editor

  1. Go to the Roblox.com Website on the Acoount you want to Edit on
  2. Click on Grow a Garden Do Not Play
  3. Right Click on the play button and click "Inspect" and Go to the Network Tab
  4. Refresh the page then in the Network Tab scroll all the way up until you see Grow a Garden
  5. Right-Click it then go to Copy -> Copy as PowerShell
  6. Enter the Copied Game Script into the Texbox above
  7. Your Done! go through and change how much Schekles and Honey you want.
